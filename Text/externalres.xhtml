External Resources

This Compendium is at it's early stages of development, and as such is very lacking in breadth. What it does cover, it covers with attention to certain criteria previously neglected - however, it doesn't yet cover very much. As such, to make up for this deficit, this section of the Compendium is to provide links to external resources for the research on fallacies. Not only can this help compensate for it's deficit in breadth - but even in the future, when this Compendium is more complete, it still won't hurt to have other sources to refer to.

Unfortunately, no such links can be provided to external links for malconducts - because (to the authorship's knowledge) the mere fact that malconducts are discussed as a category is one of the new innovations of this particular Compendium.
