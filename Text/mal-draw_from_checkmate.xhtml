Draw from Checkmate

Malconducts

Basic Description

The Draw from Checkmate is a subtype of Pigeon Chess in which one participant in the discussion (Person A) who is in fact committing the Pidgeon Chess malconduct attempts to extract zirself from the discussion in a way that makes it seem as though zir opponent (Person B) is the one committing the Pidgeon Chess malconduct.

Zie is exact as possible where in the external material Person B needs to look to find the source of the argument.
Zie references the exteranl material in addition to providing the argument in the discourse itself - not instead of it.
