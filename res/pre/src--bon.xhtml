The Bonconducts

The bonconducts are conducts or behaviors that are conducive to proper discourse, and which the Rules of Discourse therefore smile upon.

It should be noted that the distinction between malconducts and bonconducts is not about social etiquette. Social etiquette is not what is at stake if malconducts are permitted - nor is social etiquette the criterion for determining whether something is a malconduct or a bonconduct. This is squarely about assuring as well as possible an environment of proper discourse based on reason and comprehensive logic.
